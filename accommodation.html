Booking Tips

1

Book in Advance

Popular lodges and camps, especially during peak season (July-October), fill up months ahead. Book 6-12 months early for best availability.

2

Consider All-Inclusive

Many safari lodges offer all-inclusive packages covering meals, game drives, and park fees. Often better value than paying separately.

3

Check Location

Ensure your accommodation is well-located for your planned activities. Consider proximity to national parks, beaches, or airports.

4

Read Reviews

Check recent reviews on multiple platforms. Look for comments about service quality, food, wildlife sightings, and value for money.
