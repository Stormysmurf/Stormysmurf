Essential Travel Information

Visa Requirements

Most visitors can obtain an e-Visa online before traveling to Kenya. The process typically takes 2-7 business days.

  • Apply online at www.ecitizen.go.ke
  • Single entry visa: $51 USD
  • Transit visa: $21 USD

Best Time to Visit

Kenya can be visited year-round, but timing depends on your interests:

  • July-October: Great Migration in Maasai Mara
  • January-February: Dry season, excellent wildlife viewing
  • December-March: Best beach weather
  • June-October: Coolest months, ideal for trekking

Health & Safety

  • Recommended vaccinations: Yellow fever, Hepatitis A, Typhoid
  • Malaria prophylaxis advised for most regions
  • Travel insurance strongly recommended
  • Tap water not safe to drink - use bottled water

Currency & Budget

  • Currency: Kenyan Shilling (KES)
  • USD widely accepted in tourist areas
  • Credit cards accepted in major hotels and restaurants
  • Budget: $50-300+ per day depending on accommodation
